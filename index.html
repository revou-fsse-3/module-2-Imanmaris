OUR SERVICES

Electroplating

The metal coating process uses electric current and certain chemical compounds to transfer coating metal particles to the material to be coated, can prevent corrosion, improve wear resistance, electrical conductivity, light reflectivity, and improve aesthetics.

Anodizing

This process uses electrochemical principles to form a film layer of Al2O3 (aluminum oxide) on the surface of aluminum and aluminum alloys. This oxide film layer has special characteristics, such as protection, decoration, insulation and abrasion resistance.
